PB-MOT is a research project on online 3D multi-object tracking for robotics and autonomous driving. The work focuses on improving data association by incorporating pose-aware cues into the tracking pipeline.

The paper was accepted by the 2025 IEEE/RSJ International Conference on Intelligent Robots and Systems (IROS).

**Publication:** [PB-MOT: Pose-aware Association Boosted Online 3D Multi-Object Tracking](/publication/2025-10-19-pb-mot)
